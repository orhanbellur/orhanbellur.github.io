About

Mission-driven research at the intersection of computation and medicine.

I am a Ph.D. researcher in Computational Biology at the Technical University of Munich, working in collaboration with Helmholtz Munich. My research focuses on developing network- and signature-based computational frameworks for drug repositioning and biomarker discovery in neurodegenerative diseases.

I enjoy translating complex multi-omics data into actionable insights for clinicians and drug discovery teams.

Network-first discovery

Integrating systems biology with pharmacology to identify repositioning targets.

Multi-omics pipelines

Scalable workflows for harmonizing transcriptomics, proteomics, and metabolomics.

Translational impact

Bridging computational insights with experimental validation and clinical context.
